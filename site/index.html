About This Dashboard

This dashboard visualizes the relationship between Social Determinants of Health (SDOH)—including poverty, unemployment, and education levels—and healthcare utilization as measured by emergency room visits. Data is sourced from the CDC Social Vulnerability Index and synthetic FHIR patient records.
